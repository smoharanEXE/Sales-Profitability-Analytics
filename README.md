Sales & Profitability Analytics Dashboard

Project Overview

This project analyzes 50,000+ transactional sales records to evaluate revenue trends, profitability, and pricing impact. A Power BI dashboard was developed to provide executive-level insights and support data-driven decision-making.


Key Objectives

* Analyze revenue and profit trends over time
* Evaluate category and sub-category performance
* Identify drivers of margin erosion
* Provide actionable business insights



Tools & Technologies

* SQL (PostgreSQL) – Data extraction and analysis
* Power BI – Dashboard development and visualization
* Excel – Data validation and preprocessing



Dashboard Structure

🔹 1. Sales & Profitability Overview

* KPI metrics: Total Revenue, Total Profit, Profit Margin (%)
* Monthly revenue trend analysis
* Category-level revenue performance

🔹 2. Product & Profitability Analysis

* Sub-category level profit and margin analysis
* Identification of underperforming product segments
* Comparative performance breakdown

🔹 3. Discount Impact on Profitability

* Analysis of discount vs average profit
* Identification of pricing inefficiencies
* Evaluation of margin erosion drivers


Key Insights

* Overall profit margin remained stable at approximately **19%**
* Sub-category performance showed minimal variation in margins
* Higher discount levels significantly reduced profitability
* Discounting identified as the primary driver of margin erosion
